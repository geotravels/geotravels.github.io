Stargazing/Camping at OBX

If you happen to live in the 757 or want to make the drive, OBX is one of the best dark spots on the East Coast (aka you can see a LOT of stars here!).

You can see the milky way with your own eyes!

ALSO make sure you go during a clear night and new moon phase in order to get the ideal stargazing conditions! The Milky way comes out in the summer for the Northern Hemisphere!

I camp at Oregon Inlet Campground, where I spend around $28 a night. It's cheap with friends!

You can literally walk to the beach at the campground and see stars! Here are some photos I took at Oregon Inlet Campground's beach!

If you're looking for a beach/stargazing trip in the summer, this is the best place to go!!!

For star photography, try to do low aperture, 24mm to 50mm length, focus to infinity, 1600-6400 ISO and 10-30 second shutter speed! Youtube also has a lovely amount of tutorials on it.
