The 1st place was Hartman Rocks where we camped! Lovely shot of the sunrise and scenery. There's a link on it on Day 1.

The 2nd and 3rd photos are the Blue Lake trail! The lake was incredible. My friends decided to climb Mt Sneffels. I decided to stay back in order to not pass out/throw up/worse headache.

The Blue Lakes Trail was around 4 miles, with 2500 feet elevation gain. It was a little more getting to Mt Sneffels.

After a long day, we camped HERE. Here are the GPS Coordiantes: (38.002450, -107.812203)

This campsite was FREE. It had an incredible view, but get there early since it's first come first serve.
