Iceland!

On March 4-9, 2018, I went to Iceland for my school spring break! I went with my friends Sam Miller, Cindy Yu and Brad Lanza!!!

We did tons of hikes, saw waterfalls, and got lucky enough to see the Northern Lights!!

It is also very cold in Iceland, and it is wild weather. It would snow then be sunny within 30 minutes. Be prepared!

I used WOWAir (through Google Flights)! I flew from BWI (Baltimore) -> KEF! Here's an example of the flight information I grabbed from my spring break dates in 2019.

Since it is a smaller airline, it costs extra to get baggage and seat choice. If you took the flight with just a backpack and random seat choice, the total would have been 209.98! Try to book the flight 1-2 months ahead!

I rented a car with rentalcars.com. Here is the information on the website and potential cars you can rent!

My group put rental insurance, so it cost a little extra. It was pretty cheap considering the price was split between 4 people. We actually got the Polo and named it George.

Cost awares!!

Food is very expensive in Iceland. I was spending around $20 per meal, so we ate small meals (hot dogs & snacks) for breakfast and lunch, then eat dinner at places.

Gas is also expensive in Iceland, so be aware for spending money on that.

I also spent money on drinks and souvenirs, so you can save money by avoiding these. Your choice!
