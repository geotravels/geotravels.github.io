As a note, you can plug in the GPS coordinates on your maps to find these places, or click on the link. I suggest writing down these names or saving these places somewhere.

We got hot dogs in a town called Hella, highly recommend hot dogs they are cheap.

The 1st photo is HERE. GPS Coordinates (63.676367,-19.949563). The 1st photo is a giant hill to hike, with an AMAZING view.

The 2nd photo is HERE. GPS Coordinates (63.616038,-19.994102). Super cool waterfall!

The 3rd photo is HERE. GPS Coordinates (63.620849,-19.986563). Another super cool waterfall!

Don't exactly remember where the 4th photo was, but it was a nice stop. There are around 10 million puffins in Iceland? I think?

The 5th photo is the very lovely cottage we stayed at. It is called North Star Cottage. It was AMAZING and so peaceful.

The 6th photo is HERE. GPS Coordinates (63.531113, -19.478080). It's a hidden waterfall! Very cool!

The 7th photo is on the way to the 6th photo. We happened to spot horses and they approached us! They were friendly.

After a pretty long day of hikes and sightseeing, we stayed at this lovely cottage. It was $77.50 for two nights per person. We booked with booking.com, but here is the link to the place: HERE
