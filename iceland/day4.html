On our final day, we decided to explore downtown Reykjavik. We hit stores and a few bars.

After spending time in Reykjavik, we decided to go back one more time to see the northern lights. We kept using this website to find out about the northern lights.

When we went out, it had a 0-1 aurora forecast. We went anyway.

We saw the northern lights an hour north from Reykjavik. It was in the middle of nowhere, and I don't remember exactly where we went.

You can find a place to see northern lights by driving north until it's very dark. Just try to get an open view of the sky and you're good.

Northern lights are very unpredictable. Our group got lucky and saw them, but it is all chance.

After seeing the northern lights and staying up till 3am, we went back and slept at this hostel. Our on 5th day, we just flew back to USA.
